## Framework hooks

- **Every FOMC is a Regulatory Phase 5 decision point.** A rate cut or clear dovish turn against a 4%+ CPI print reads as political capture overriding the price-stability mandate — a hard Phase 5 confirmation. After the June meeting, the September and December SEP meetings carry the most signal.
- **CPI feeds the inflation line** — WARN >3% / ALERT >4% YoY on both headline and core. The May print (Jun 10) is the first test of whether the energy shock has pushed headline above the 4% ALERT.
- **Jobs feed two channels** — the unemployment WARN >4.5% threshold (held at 4.3% through the May print), and the rate-path repricing that has been pulling the 10-year Treasury toward its 4.75% WARN.
- **Pairings matter for the convergence test.** When a CPI or jobs print and an FOMC fall in the same week (June 10 CPI → June 16–17 FOMC), the data loads the meeting directly; treat the pair as a single binary rather than two independent events.
